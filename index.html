Our Mission

✦   ✦   ✦

"לֹא יָמוּשׁ סֵפֶר הַתּוֹרָה הַזֶּה מִפִּיךָ"
"This book of Torah shall not depart from your mouth" — Yehoshua 1:8

Simanim was built by a group of people passionate about making all of Nach truly knowable — not just studied, but remembered. Using proven memory techniques, we're creating simanim for every sefer in Nach, one book at a time.

Our vision is simple: anyone, of any age, should be able to hold the entire Nach in their mind. As the project grows, we plan to expand these tools to all of Torah.
